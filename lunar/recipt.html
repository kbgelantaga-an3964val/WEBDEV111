Our Story

Founded in 2026, Luna Vista was built on the dream of merging modern luxury with the raw beauty of the Palawan coastline.

Our Mission


To provide an unparalleled sanctuary for travelers seeking peace, personalized service, and sustainable luxury. We believe every guest deserves a view of the moon over the vista.

Sustainable Luxury


Luna Vista operates with a zero-plastic policy and utilizes solar-powered energy for all our beachfront villas to ensure the environment remains as pristine as our service.

Meet the Team

Our dedicated staff is available 24/7 to ensure your stay is perfect. From our world-class chefs to our local island guides, we are here for you.
